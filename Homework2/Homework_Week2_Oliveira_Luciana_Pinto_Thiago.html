What is Eco-system?

Eco-system is a web-based software developed exclusively for companies in the recycling industry and has the exact tools that enables recyclers to manage their businesses more efficiently.

Why should your recycling company adopt the Eco-system?

 Easy to use

Eco-system has the basic functionalities that allows your team to manage your recycling company and an easy to understand layout.

 Allows for autonomy

Different people from you team can work at the same time in the system or can work from any computer with acess to internet.

  It is free!

You can have as many people using the system as you would like. The acesses are all free of charges!

What are Eco-system's funcionalities?

Insert selling receipts
Integrate with receipts from suppliers
Allows for multiple acesses
Automaticly generated dashboards

No waste left behind. No waste picker left behind.

New Hope Ecotech is a social start-up that connects manufacturers with a low income grow of recyclers in Brazil. We help manufacturers pay for the the independent recyclers' work. As a result, independent recyclers can have an honest wage for an honest effort

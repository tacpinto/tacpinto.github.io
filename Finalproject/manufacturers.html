Environment Service Credits for Manufacturers

New Hope Ecotech is a technology solution that leverages the informal recycling flow to create a formal pathway for manufacturers to fulfil their government mandate. We track the recycling activity and guarantees that mandatory recycling has been met by issuing a reverse logistics certification. These certificates enable manufacturers to legally fulfil their government mandate.

Here's why we offers the best solution for reverse logistic investments

 

 

 Economic

New Hope Ecotech's database allows for precise investment in accordance to the real amount of materials your company sold to the marketplace.

 

 

 Transparent

Thanks to the clound-based software used by recyclers, Manufacturers can track in real time the amount of materials recycled.

 

 

  Inclusive

Our software was adopted by a broad variety of independent recyclers who needs your support to keep the good work.
